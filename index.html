Meet Jeanne Rowe.

Sixteen-year-old Jeanne Rowe finds her life in shambles after the death of her mother. Hurt and confused about the sudden downturn in her life, she drowns herself in booze and drugs in hopes that it’ll all just fade away.

It doesn’t.

Then there’s Ryan, the boy who just won’t stay away. He’s intent on gaining her trust, but Jeanne isn’t the trusting type–hasn’t been for years. “Ignore him and he’ll go away” is her motto… until she starts wanting him to stay.
